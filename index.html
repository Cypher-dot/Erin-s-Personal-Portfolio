About Me

I’m an economist with a double master’s from UCL and experience in data analysis, economic research, and business insights. My expertise lies in Python, R, Tableau and Power BI with a strong passion for uncovering patterns in data.

Most recently I was part of an employer project as a Data Analyst at the Bank of England, I applied structured thinking to distil complex business problems, utilised advanced software like Excel, SQL, Tableau, Python, and R for data interrogation, and presented actionable insights to stakeholders. My role involves significant collaboration with diverse professionals, enhancing my teamwork and project management skills.

In my academic journey, I completed a double master's in Economics and Business from University College London, achieving a merit classification (2:1), and conducted research in Prague with the support of an Erasmus scholarship. My undergraduate studies in Economics at the University of Kerala placed me in the top 5% of my class with an 8.87 GPA. While at London South Bank University and London Metropolitan University, I served as a Lecturer and Module Lead, mentoring over 100 students and innovating curricula to improve student engagement and understanding.

I have also contributed as a Research Assistant at UCL, where I honed my market research and analytical skills, and as an Educator at Kalendit, demonstrating my ability to manage and deliver tailored learning experiences. Additionally, my role as a Student Advisor for Sustainability at UCL and Camden Council showcased my creative problem-solving and communication abilities in addressing environmental challenges.

I recently completed a Career Accelerator certification course in Data Analytics from the London School of Economics and Political Science, further enhancing my analytical capabilities. My technical proficiency includes Microsoft Office, Stata, Tableau, Python, and SQL.

A passion for data-driven decision-making and economic research drives my career. I am committed to leveraging my skills and experiences to contribute to impactful projects and innovative solutions in the fields of data analysis and economics. Outside of work, I am deeply interested in music, public speaking, and environmental causes.
